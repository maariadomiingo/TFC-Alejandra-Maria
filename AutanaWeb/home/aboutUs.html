Behind the Threads

The people and passion behind Autana's artisanal creations

Our Story

Autana was born from Daniela’s final project in Fashion Design and Marketing at Istituto Marangoni, but it’s much more than that. It’s a tribute to our roots, a celebration of our personal journey, and a love letter to Amazonas — the land where our story began. Through Autana, we bring to life the spirit, beauty, and strength of our homeland, weaving its essence into every design.

The Vision

Founded by Daniela Duarte Abdul Khalek, Autana is a heartfelt tribute to our roots in Amazonas. It embodies the spirit of our homeland — its vibrant culture, rich traditions, and deep connection to nature. Through each design, we tell a story of heritage and identity, blending ancestral craftsmanship with contemporary style to inspire and honor where we come from.

The Name

Autana takes its name from the majestic Tepui mountain in Venezuela, symbolizing our connection to nature, indigenous wisdom, and the aspiration to elevate traditional craftsmanship to new heights.

Our Core Values

Cultural Preservation

We honor and sustain traditional techniques that might otherwise disappear, ensuring artisans receive fair recognition and compensation.

Sustainable Practices

From natural dyes to organic materials, every element is chosen with environmental impact in mind.

Modern Elegance

We reinterpret traditional designs for contemporary wardrobes, creating timeless pieces with cultural depth.

Artisan Collaborations

At the heart of Autana are the skilled artisans who bring each design to life. We work directly with craftspeople from indigenous communities, preserving their techniques while ensuring fair wages and working conditions.

Every purchase supports these traditional crafts and helps sustain cultural heritage for future generations.
